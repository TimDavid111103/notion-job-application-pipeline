# Jack Inbox Prompts

These are example prompts to use as inspiration when filling the inbox. Do not send them verbatim or in order — adapt, combine, and vary them based on what kinds of roles are still missing from the inbox. The goal is variety across role type, location, and company context while always landing on junior/associate/new grad AI engineering roles.

---

Find junior or new grad AI engineer roles at US companies building agentic workflows, LLM pipelines, or autonomous task automation. Must sponsor H-1B or OPT. 0–2 years experience welcome.

Search for associate or entry-level roles where I'd be building internal AI tooling, workflow automation, or LLM-powered productivity features for employees. US-based, visa sponsorship required (H-1B or OPT). New grad welcome.

Find new grad or junior roles focused on RAG pipelines, vector search, or retrieval-augmented generation at AI startups or scale-ups. H-1B or OPT sponsorship required.

Look for junior AI engineer or associate software engineer roles at seed or Series A AI-native startups. Focus on companies building agentic products, copilots, or autonomous agents. Must sponsor H-1B or OPT. Open to new grads.

Find entry-level or associate AI developer roles at companies in a non-AI sector — fintech, healthcare, legal tech, or enterprise SaaS — where the role is specifically about building AI features, LLM integrations, or automation pipelines. H-1B or OPT sponsorship required.

Search for junior or new grad roles at companies where I'd be building or maintaining evaluation frameworks, observability tooling, or production LLM systems. Visa sponsorship required.

Find junior or associate AI engineer roles in the UK or Germany at companies building agentic AI products, LLM orchestration layers, or AI-powered internal tools. Visa sponsorship required. New grad or 0–2 years experience welcome.

Search for new grad or entry-level roles in Europe — UK, Germany, Netherlands, or Ireland — where I'd be working on RAG systems, AI pipelines, or workflow automation. Must offer visa sponsorship. No senior roles.

Find junior AI engineer or associate software engineer roles at early-stage AI startups in London, Berlin, or Amsterdam. Company should be building agentic AI or autonomous workflow products. Visa sponsorship required. New grad welcome.

Look for junior or associate AI developer roles at companies in sectors like fintech, healthtech, or legaltech where the role is specifically about building LLM integrations or AI-powered internal tools. Visa sponsorship required.

Find new grad or entry-level roles at companies where I'd be building internal automation tools, AI-powered ops tooling, or LLM-backed productivity features for non-technical teams. Role can be anywhere with visa sponsorship. Junior or associate level only.

Search for junior or associate agentic AI engineer roles at companies building multi-agent systems, orchestration layers, or autonomous decision-making pipelines. Open to US or Europe. Visa sponsorship required. No senior or lead roles.

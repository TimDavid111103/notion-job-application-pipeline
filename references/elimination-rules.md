# Elimination Rules

Quick-skim rules for discarding roles before logging. Apply these at a glance during the review pass — do not do a deep read to confirm. The goal is to move fast, not to do a thorough analysis. **When in doubt, keep the role.** Borderline cases pass through to scoring.

Discard a role only if **any** of the following is obvious on a quick look:

1. **Wrong domain** — the role is clearly not related to AI Engineering, Software Engineering, or Agentic AI/workflows. Examples of clear discards: finance analyst, marketing coordinator, sales representative, operations manager with no software component. If there is any plausible connection to software or AI, keep it.

2. **Explicitly senior-level** — the word "Senior," "Staff," "Principal," or "Lead" appears in the job title, or the description explicitly describes it as a senior-level role. Do not infer seniority from listed responsibilities or tech requirements — only discard if the seniority label is stated outright.

3. **Explicitly unpaid** — the posting explicitly states the role is unpaid or offers no compensation (e.g., "volunteer," "no salary," "stipend only").

**Default: keep.** If none of the above is obviously true from a quick glance, log the role and move on.
